Key reminders

  1. Do NOT accept the early release lump sum — stay on payroll via unwiderrufliche Freistellung for all 7 months. Health insurance, pension contributions, and ALG I reference salary all suffer if you leave early.
  2. Section IV of your contract is legally binding — insist every calculation uses 22 October 2008 as the start date. The word angerechnet is unambiguous in German law.
  3. Severance and leave payout are taxed differently — severance benefits from the Fünftelregelung; leave payout (Urlaubsabgeltung) does not. Never split severance across two tax years.
  4. Two separate registrations with Agentur für Arbeit — arbeitssuchend within 3 days of receiving notice, then arbeitslos on your last actual working day.
